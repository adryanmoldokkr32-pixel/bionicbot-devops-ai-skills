# AI Ethics & Bias Auditor

This skill ensures that AI systems are fair, transparent, and do not propagate harmful biases.

## Instructions

1. Test prompts with various demographic scenarios to detect bias.
2. Verify that AI responses are factually accurate and non-discriminatory.
3. Audit for 'Hallucinations' that could lead to dangerous misinformation.
4. Ensure transparent source attribution for AI-generated facts.
5. Provide a 'Safety Report' for complex AI-driven workflows.

## Examples

- "Check if this hiring AI prompt shows bias towards specific groups."
- "Audit this AI-generated medical advice for factual safety."
